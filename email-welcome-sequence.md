## SEQUENCE NOTES

### Exit Conditions
- Lead replies to any email (move to manual conversation)
- Lead clicks founders link and completes purchase (move to onboarding sequence)
- Lead unsubscribes

### Tagging Strategy (MailerLite)
| Tag | Trigger |
|-----|---------|
| Welcome Sequence Active | Email 1 sent |
| Welcome Sequence Complete | Email 5 sent |
| Clicked Demo | Any demo link click |
| Clicked Founders | Any founders link click |
| Email Replied | Manual tag when reply received |

### Copy Rules Applied
- No em dashes (used -- where needed)
- One CTA per email (demo link OR founders link, never both)
- Signed off with "-- Cole" or "-- Cole Cummings"
- Conversational, first person, blue-collar voice
- Short paragraphs throughout
- No corporate speak ("leverage", "optimize", "synergy" never appear)
- Steel worker angle referenced in every email

### Performance Benchmarks
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Open rate | 35%+ (subject lines are curiosity-driven and specific) |
| Click rate | 5%+ |
| Reply rate | 2%+ |
| Sequence-to-demo conversion | 10%+ |
| Sequence-to-founder conversion | 3%+ |
