What kvCORE Does Well

kvCORE (by Inside Real Estate) is one of the most feature-complete platforms in real estate. It combines a CRM, IDX website, lead routing, transaction management, and marketing automation into one package.

For teams and brokerages, the biggest advantage is that many brokerages offer kvCORE for free as part of their agent benefits. If your brokerage provides it, that is genuine value at no extra cost.

The CRM is robust with smart campaigns, behavioral automation, and pipeline tracking. The IDX website gives buyers a solid search experience. And the dialer and texting features help agents follow up on leads efficiently.

Where HomeEdge Is Different

kvCORE tries to do everything. HomeEdge focuses on one thing: making listing agents more productive with AI.

kvCORE does not write your listing descriptions. It does not generate CMA reports, create social media posts for specific properties, or draft client emails with showing feedback. These are the tasks that eat hours out of a listing agent's week.

HomeEdge has 28 AI tools built specifically for these workflows. You type in property details and get professional, ready-to-use output in seconds. Everything is white-labeled to your brand.

Many agents who use kvCORE through their brokerage add HomeEdge on top. kvCORE handles the CRM and lead management. HomeEdge handles the listing productivity. They complement each other well.
