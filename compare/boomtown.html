What BoomTown Does Well

BoomTown (now part of Inside Real Estate) has been in the real estate tech space for over a decade. They have built a strong reputation for lead generation through paid advertising, especially Google PPC campaigns.

Their lead quality tends to be higher than many competitors because of their focus on intent-based search advertising. When someone searches "homes for sale in [your market]" and clicks a BoomTown ad, that is a motivated buyer.

The CRM is purpose-built for real estate with smart lead routing, automated follow-up sequences, and accountability tracking for teams. Their "Success Assurance" program also provides ISA-like services to nurture leads until they are ready to engage.

For teams that need a steady pipeline of buyer leads, BoomTown delivers consistent results.

Where HomeEdge Is Different

BoomTown is built for teams buying leads. HomeEdge is built for agents working listings.

BoomTown does not help you write a listing description, analyze comps for a CMA, generate social media content for a new listing, or draft a professional seller update email. These are the daily tasks that listing agents need help with.

HomeEdge has 28 AI tools that handle exactly these workflows. You spend less time on paperwork and more time with clients. Every tool is white-labeled so your sellers see polished, professional materials under your brand.

The cost difference is significant. BoomTown requires $750+/month for the platform plus $1,000+/month in recommended ad spend. HomeEdge costs $49/month with no additional spending required.

Who Should Choose What

Choose BoomTown if: You are a team or brokerage that needs a steady pipeline of buyer leads and you have the budget for both the platform fee and significant monthly ad spend. BoomTown works best when you invest heavily in advertising and have agents ready to work the leads.

Choose HomeEdge if: You are a listing agent who wants to be more productive and professional without spending thousands per month. You want AI tools that save you hours every week on the repetitive parts of listing work. You want to start today with minimal risk.

These platforms solve completely different problems. BoomTown generates leads. HomeEdge makes you better at servicing listings. If you have the budget, using both gives you lead flow and listing efficiency.
