What Ylopo Does Well

Ylopo is a serious platform. They have built one of the best Facebook and Google ad systems in real estate. Their dynamic ads that showcase individual listings are genuinely impressive, and their AI assistant "rAIya" handles lead follow-up automatically.

If your primary need is paid lead generation at scale and you have the budget for both the platform and the ad spend, Ylopo delivers real results. Teams and brokerages with large ad budgets often see strong ROI.

Their IDX website integration is also solid, giving buyers a clean search experience tied directly to your ad campaigns.

Where HomeEdge Is Different

HomeEdge is not a lead generation platform. It is a productivity platform for listing agents.

Where Ylopo helps you buy leads, HomeEdge helps you work smarter with the clients you already have. You get 28 AI tools that handle the time-consuming parts of running listings: writing descriptions, building CMAs, creating social posts, drafting client emails, calculating net proceeds, and more.

Every tool is white-labeled to your brand. Your sellers never see HomeEdge. They just see an agent with incredibly polished materials and lightning-fast turnaround.

The biggest difference is the barrier to entry. HomeEdge costs $49/month with no setup fee, no contract, and no ad spend required. You can start using it today and cancel anytime.
