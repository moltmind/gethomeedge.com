## TOP 5 FACEBOOK GROUPS TO POST IN

Target groups with high membership, active engagement, and a focus on listing agents or real estate marketing. Avoid groups that ban self-promotion outright. The gift bot angle works because you're OFFERING something free, not selling.

### 1. Real Estate Agent Round Table
- **Members:** 140,000+
- **Why:** One of the largest active agent groups. High engagement on tech and marketing posts. Moderators allow value-driven posts that aren't direct ads.

### 2. Listing Agent Lifestyle
- **Members:** 50,000+
- **Why:** Specifically focused on listing agents. This is the exact audience. Posts about listing appointment tools get strong engagement here.

### 3. Real Estate Mastermind (by Jason Wardrop or similar)
- **Members:** 80,000+
- **Why:** Tech-forward agents who already use tools and are open to AI. They understand the value proposition faster than traditional agents.

### 4. Lab Coat Agents
- **Members:** 160,000+
- **Why:** Massive group with strong engagement. Agents here actively discuss tools, tech stacks, and competitive advantages. The "show don't tell" approach fits perfectly.

### 5. Real Estate Marketing / Tech Groups (search for active ones with 20K+ members)
- **Members:** Varies (20,000-60,000)
- **Why:** Groups specifically about real estate marketing and technology. Members are already looking for tools. Your "free demo" offer will stand out because most posts in these groups are people ASKING for solutions, not offering them.

## POSTING STRATEGY

**Timing:** Post between 7-9am local time on weekdays. Agents check Facebook before their first showing.

**Frequency:** One post per group per week. Rotate between the 3 posts. Never post the same thing in the same group twice in a row.

**Week 1 Schedule:**
- Monday: Post 1 (Screen Recording) in Groups 1 and 2
- Wednesday: Post 2 (Results) in Groups 3 and 4
- Friday: Post 3 (Before/After) in Group 5

**Week 2:** Rotate. Post 2 goes to Groups 1 and 2. Post 3 goes to 3 and 4. Post 1 goes to 5.

**Comment Management:** When agents comment their city or brokerage, reply within 1 hour. Say: "Got it. Building yours now. I'll DM you when it's ready." Then actually build the demo and send it. Speed is the differentiator.

**DM Follow-up:** After sending the demo, wait 24 hours then send: "Hey [Name], did you get a chance to play with the demo? Curious what you think. No pressure either way."

---

## WHY THIS WORKS

1. **No selling.** You're giving something away. Facebook groups don't flag gift offers.
2. **Product sells itself.** Once an agent sees their name on a chatbot that actually works, they want it.
3. **Social proof compounds.** Every comment ("Drop your city") makes the post look more popular. Facebook's algorithm pushes posts with more comments.
4. **Low friction.** "Comment your city" is easier than "click this link." You get the lead AND the algorithm boost.
5. **Builds authority.** You're the guy who builds AI tools for agents. Not the guy who sells them. Big difference in how agents perceive you.
