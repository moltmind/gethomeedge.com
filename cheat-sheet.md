- **AI generators** -- CMAs, listing descriptions, social media posts, follow-up emails, video scripts, pricing strategy, seller updates, objection handling, expired listing outreach, neighborhood farming, testimonial requests, market analysis
- **Smart calculators** -- net proceeds, fix vs. sell, commission value, closing costs, staging ROI, offer comparison, home value tracker
- **Dashboards** -- seller portal, lead CRM, performance analytics, open house QR system, listing portfolio, pre-list checklist
- **AI chatbot** -- answers seller questions on your site 24/7

### "How is this different from what I already have?"
Your current site is a brochure. HomeEdge is a working platform. When a seller visits your site at 11pm with a question, your chatbot answers it. When you're at a listing appointment, you pull up the net proceeds calculator on THEIR home right there at the kitchen table. No other agent in your market has this.

### "Is it really my brand? Will sellers see HomeEdge?"
100% your brand. Your logo, your colors, your domain. Sellers never see HomeEdge. It looks like you built it yourself.

### "How long to set up?"
48 hours from when you send us your logo and colors. We do all the work.

### "Do I need to be tech savvy?"
No. If you can open a link, you can use it. We handle all the tech.
